    OUR PRODUCTION

    Our production produces parts using the Vacuum Assisted Infusion (VAI) technology, if you haven't heard of it before, it is the production of composite parts using vacuum. The specified composition of fibers is placed in the prepared mold, then it is covered with a tarpaulin from below, which sucks up all the air content and creates a vacuum. Once the part is ready in this way, it begins to flow into the resin mold through the filling valve. The part is allowed to harden and thus the production process ends. With this technology, it is possible to achieve a high-quality laminate with a fiber ratio of up to 70%. For laymen, this means that the part will be strong and light. It is characterized as the most complex process of manufacturing composite materials.

    Contact lamination

    The fabric is folded into a mold and at the same time impregnated with resin. We use vacuum suction to achieve a better product and optimal saturation of the fabric.

    MATERIALS

    Fabrics used for the production of parts:

    Carbon fabrics

    We use high quality fabrics for our products. Products made of this material are characterized by high strength and very low weight. The type of fiber weave in the fabric determines the final appearance of the product and also affects its physical properties.

    Aramid (Kevlar) fabrics

    Aramid fibers are a special group of synthetic fibers which, due to their chemical properties, are particularly strong and resistant to high temperatures. Aramid provides very high resistance to shocks, abrasion and is much more resistant to breakage. Aramid fabric is available in weave patterns:


    - Twill

    - Sateen

    Hybrid (Carbon-Kevlar) fabrics

    This material combines the properties of two fabrics - carbon fiber interwoven with Kevlar fiber. Carbon excels in high levels of stiffness and strength, while Aramid provides resistance to impact, abrasion and breakage. Hybrid fabric is used for products in demanding conditions.

    Colored fabrics

    These special fabrics are made by combining carbon fiber and colored polyester fiber. Colored fabrics give each piece an amazing and unique look. Colored fabrics are only in Twill weave and three different color variants:


    - Blue Carbon

    - Red Carbon

    - Green Carbon
